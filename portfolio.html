InClinico

InClinico is a platform for Data-driven multimodal forecast of a single clinical trial's probability of success. It utilizes massive amounts of data about the targets, diseases, clinical trials, and scientists involved with the study at the preclinical and clinical stages. Scoring of custom trials: compare designs for the clinical trial that are planned and optimize them for success. Database of historical and ongoing clinical trials: find clinical catalysts that drive pharmaceutical growth, sort them by the PoS, and research the designs of successful trials. Clinical Trial Outcome Scores: the ability to analyze performance for individual trials with simple data-driven metrics for the PoS.

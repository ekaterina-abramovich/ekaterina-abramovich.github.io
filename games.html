Foxy Adventures

It is my very first game made in Unity. The game is created in 2D format and consists of one level. Graphics are made in a pixilated style. The player is a fox who has to reach the end by avoiding different obstacles and enemies. There is no time limit, however, the player has only one attempt, and in case of a collision with enemies or obstacles will have to start the level over.

Become

A 3D game that consists of 4 levels with 1st and 3rd person playing formats. A player is a peasant whose wife was kidnapped by Evil. The goal is to make a potion that will turn the player into a dragon, then fly to the castle by slaughtering Evil's army, and save the kidnapped girl. The project was built using Universal Render Pipeline and has quite realistic assets.

Feed Your Gran

A short horror survival 1st person 3D game made in Unity. A player walks around the dark house in search of ingredients for apple pie by avoiding the evil grandma who is wandering the house too. While at first, the player should avoid the old lady after all ingredients are put in one place the goal becomes the opposite - the player must find grandma and feed her!

Upside Down

A 2D platform video game where a player has to bring two cubes divided from each other to exit within a limited amount of time. Even though two levels are visible simultaneously, cubes may only be controlled by turn. While the first cube should jump up the 2nd one has to go down. There are enemies which will send the cubes back at the beginning in case of collision.

InClinico

InClinico is a platform for data-driven multimodal forecasts of clinical trials’ probability of success. It utilizes massive amounts of data about the targets, diseases, clinical trials, and scientists involved with the study at the preclinical and clinical stages. It can compare designs for the clinical trial that are planned and optimize them for success, find clinical catalysts that drive pharmaceutical growth, sort them by the PoS, and research the designs of successful trials, analyze performance for individual trials with simple data-driven metrics.
